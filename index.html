Previously, I got a MSc in Artificial Intelligence from Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya and wrote a Master’s Thesis on Trust-Aware Recommender Systems advised by Ricard Gavaldà.

Before that, I studied Telecommunication Engineering and worked for seven years at Telefónica I+D (a.k.a Telefonica Research) as a research engineer. I am also a member of the Datanalysis15m research group. (Universitat Oberta de Catalunya).

My main research interests are Statistical Machine Learning, Data Mining and Social Network Analysis
